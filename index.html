#Onemillionfaces

The #Onemillionfaces project is a campaign under the Project One Productions program for the year 2021. Project One is an organisation that promotes, patriotism, integrity, pride in national identity and self-reliance.

We have embarked on the #Onemillionfaces campaign to lend our voices to the unity of Nigeria and our faces represent the unity of a people from many walks of life, culture, religious background and orientation. What binds us together is our green, white, green and a desire to see the Nigeria of our dreams.
